RUSA

Randonneurs USA (RUSA) is the affiliate of Les Randonneurs Mondiaux in the United States.

Regional Brevet Administrator

Randonneurs USA (RUSA) appoints individuals called “regional brevet administrators” to schedule and manage randonneuring events in a geographic area. Michal Young was appointed RBA for the South Willamette region (roughly Eugene and surrounding areas) beginning calendar year 2017.

Nearby Regions

Nearby regions and clubs include Oregon Randonneurs, based primarily in the Portland area but also riding in the Columbia Gorge, Willamette Valley, and Central Oregon; Seattle International Randonneurs, based in Seattle but with many rides in other parts of Washington; Desert River Randonneurs based in SE Washington and NE Oregon; and Montana Randonneurs based in Bozeman. To our south, there are several active randonneuring clubs in the SF Bay area.
