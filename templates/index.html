Willamette Randonneurs is a group of randonneurs and those interested in randonneuring in the southern Willamette Valley and surrounding areas. Willamette Randonneurs was formed in late 2016, with our first official season in calendar year 2017.

The regional brevet admistrator (RBA) for our region is Michal Young.

Informal news and announcements often appear in our Facebook group Eugene Area Randonneurs or on our Facebook page Willamette Randonneurs. For those who prefer email forums to Facebook, I will do my best to cross post to our will-rando group, and to respond to posts there.

COVID special measures spring 2021

After a hiatus, we believe we can again safely hold brevets beginning in April 2021. We are closely monitoring conditions in Oregon counties, and will make adjustments (or in the worst case, shut down again) if we believe that we cannot do so safely. In the meantime, here are measures we will adopt.

  • Riders must register 24 hours before the event. This is so that we will have an accurate count of riders and can arrange a staggered start if necessary. There will be no day-of-event registration.
  • We will depart in groups no larger than 6. Staggered start times (15 minutes apart) will be arranged if more than 6 riders have registered for a ride. When you register, please provide a telephone number at which we could reach you to arrange a start time if necessary.
  • Riders should ride alone or in groups no larger than three.
  • Riders must wear a mask at the start, and carry the mask with them to wear when entering any establishment on the ride.
  • All controls will be photo or info controls.
  • We will attempt to choose routes that make it possible to minimize contact with persons out-of-area. In some cases we will arrange drop bag service to reduce the need to shop and dine indoors.
  • There will be no gathering after the ride. Riders will be asked to phone an organizer approximately 30 minutes before the finish to arrange a safe handoff of the brevet card.

These provisions have been submitted to RUSA for approval. They will be amended or extended as recommended by RUSA.
